Mr. Vivek Alva

Discover Vivek Alva, Managing Trustee of Alva's Education Foundation, forward-thinking contributions to the creation of a stunning bird park. This story reveals his commitment to avian conservation and education, establishing a refuge where nature coexists with human advancement, through passion and effort. A fascinating trip is in store.

The Chirpp Club was founded as a result of Vivek M. Alva's great love for the preservation of wildlife and the natural world. Vivek saw the potential in using their campus, which proved to be a rich home for many bird species, when Suraj, an alumni of Alva's Institute of Engineering and Technology, approached him with the idea of starting a bird watching group.

M. Vivek Alva has made truly exceptional contributions to the Chirpp Club and the creation of the bird park Vivek also thanks Vrijulal and Chaturved, two of his closest friends, for their everlasting dedication to the club and the idea to build a bird park. Their steadfast assistance and teamwork have been crucial in removing barriers and realising the vision. Additionally, Vivek expresses his gratitude to Dr. T.V. Ramachandra and his IISc team for their guidance and support along the voyage.

Vivek Alva honours the memories and the profound effect of the late Harish Bhat Sir during the celebration of group accomplishments. The crew was motivated and dedicated because of his undying enthusiasm for birds and other species. The "Harish Bhat Pakishi Vana " shall always be called so as an homage to him and his tremendous contribution. Serving as a continual reminder of his guiding principle, Harish Bhat Pakshi Vana.

In conclusion, Vivek M. Alva's tireless work and steadfast dedication to the the creation of the bird park have resulted in an amazing haven for both birdwatchers and environment lovers. His inspiration, the commitment of the kids, and the teamwork of mentors and professionals have produced a transformational initiative that will continue to inspire future generations.

Together, let's thank Vivek M. Alva for his outstanding efforts and rejoice in the harmony. Harish Bhat Pakshi Vana, a stunning representation of the harmony between nature and man.
