"Wings & Whistles: Exploring the Enchanting Birds Park"

In this YouTube video, the speaker gives viewers a tour of the Shobhavana at AIET in Mangalore. This well-maintained garden is home to over 1400 varieties of medicinal plants, each with its own designated names. As the speaker walks through the garden, different flowers, leaves, and informative boards are showcased, providing valuable knowledge about the plants. The garden offers a serene environment for visitors to immerse themselves in nature. One plant in particular is highlighted for its beneficial properties for diabetes, leprosy, and wounds. Overall, the botanical garden at Alva College is a beautiful and informative place to explore and learn more about the diverse range of medicinal plants it hosts
