Shobhavana

Shobhavana is a garden with priceless medicinal plants dedicated in the memory of Late Smt. Shobha Alva. The Herbal plantation is spread across 100 acres of land having 58 separate vanas or sub sections, and has about 2,500 varieties of medicinal plants. Shobhavana is a certified 'Organic Plantation' and is the first of its kind 'Medicinal Garden' in entire South India. Anandamaya Arogyadhama is located inside this campus and the inmates are at liberty to walk inside this garden which is also inhabited by about 70 bird species and variety of butterflies.

Cottage Area

Nestled amidst picturesque landscapes, lies a charming cottage area where nature enthusiasts can immerse themselves in the serenity of birdwatching. Surrounded by lush greenery, visitors can relax on the cozy verandas of our cottages, savoring the melodies of birdsong and capturing breathtaking moments as colorful avian species flit and dance in the nearby habitats. This tranquil retreat offers a unique vantage point to witness the beauty of birds up close, creating an unforgettable experience for all who seek solace and connection with the natural world.

Bird and Butterfly Park

Our bird park is a lush sanctuary, adorned with diverse plants and trees that cater to the needs of a wide range of birds, including migrants. It serves as a nurturing habitat, providing abundant food, shelter, and nesting spaces for our feathered residents.

Virasat Ground

Our Virasat Ground features a unique playground zone with an elevated view, offering an ideal spot to observe a variety of birds in action. From this vantage point, visitors can enjoy clear views and delightful sounds as they encounter numerous bird species, creating a memorable experience for bird enthusiasts

Beyond Virasat ground

Beyond recreation, the natural environment also plays an important role in supporting the health and well-being of humans and other living creatures. Trees and other plants help to purify the air and provide important habitats for wildlife, while bodies of water are crucial for drinking, irrigation, and other human needs.

Pond area

A pond area typically refers to an area of land that contains one or more ponds, which are small bodies of standing water. These areas are often found in natural environments, such as forests, wetlands, or meadows, but they can also be created artificially in parks or gardens. Pond areas can provide important habitat for a variety of plants and animals. The water in a pond can support aquatic plants such as water lilies and cattails, which provide shelter and food for fish, frogs, and other aquatic animals.
